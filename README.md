# CA Final AI Tutor Prompts

A unified suite of advanced AI system prompts utilizing the **Synergy Method** and **Malayalam Analogies** for CA Final exam preparation.

## Overview
This repository contains highly engineered master prompts designed to transform standard Large Language Models (LLMs) into elite, expert-level tutors for the Chartered Accountancy (CA) Final examinations. The prompts are optimized to enforce strict adherence to ICAI (Institute of Chartered Accountants of India) standards, ensuring exact formatting, deep conceptual clarity, and high-speed exam execution.

## Included Modules

* **Advanced Auditing (v4.1):** Features the "Sticky Malayalam Analogy" & Exam Precision strictness, enforcing official SA (Standards on Auditing) naming conventions, mark-matching discipline, and definitive verdict generation.
* **Advanced Financial Management / AFM (v2.3):** Utilizes the tripartite Synergy Method, mandating precise mathematical outputs, exact Indian numbering systems (Lakhs/Crores), and step-by-step logical breakdowns.
* **Financial Reporting / FR (v3.0):** Focuses on Ind AS (Indian Accounting Standards) precision, Journal Entry extractions, and fast pattern recognition for complex case studies.

## Methodology
These system instructions bypass standard, generic AI responses by forcing the model to adopt specific pedagogical frameworks:
1. **Concept Visualization:** Translating complex financial/audit scenarios into relatable, everyday Kerala-life examples.
2. **ICAI Formatting Lock:** Ensuring all answers mirror the strict phrasing and structural expectations of the official examiner answer keys.
3. **Zero-Drift Rules:** Enforcing absolute discipline on how headers, sub-questions, and keyword emphasis (Bold-Italics) are rendered.
